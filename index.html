About the Artist

Georgina Roy is a Unique Artist who creates her work alongside the environment around her. With a degree in Graphic Design, she is influenced from her studies in Photography and Art. She thanks her Grandparents for teaching her the use of finding her own techniques to use when painting.

Most of her work is painted with acrylic, oil and water color. The techniques of that day, are used to work the ideas into the canvas and make you become more involved, via touch, vision or color. When you use light or dark, feeling, sight and sound, it can bring impact to the canvas. To think and feel the image you want to create, is very inspiring and you can bring your image to life, and be unique in the process.

We are all a canvas of opportunity waiting to share with others, what we can feel and see. She hopes her work is something you enjoy viewing and when you look at each piece of my Art work that you feel the energy of each painting, and the passion that brings it to life.

Much Love from the Artist!
